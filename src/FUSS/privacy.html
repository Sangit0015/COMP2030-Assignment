Privacy Policy

Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how FUSS (Flinders Uni Skill Share) handles personal information. It aligns with university expectations and applicable Australian privacy principles.

Information We Collect

  • Identity and contact: name, student email, student ID, profile details.
  • Platform activity: skills listed, bookings, credit transactions, messages and feedback.
  • Technical: device, browser, IP address, access times, performance and security logs.

How We Use Information

  • To operate core features including profiles, listings, bookings, credits, and messaging.
  • To support safety, moderation, troubleshooting, analytics, and service improvement.
  • To communicate essential updates about bookings, credits, or policy changes.

Sharing and Disclosure

  • Within Flinders University where required for student services and support.
  • With service providers for hosting, security, and analytics under confidentiality obligations.
  • When required by law or to address safety, misconduct, or security issues.

Security

Access to data is controlled and limited to authorised roles. Technical and organisational measures are applied to protect information. Users should protect their accounts and report concerns promptly.

Retention

Data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service, meet audit and compliance needs, and maintain accurate records. Some transactional logs may be kept longer consistent with record-keeping schedules.

Cookies and Analytics

Essential cookies and limited analytics may be used to support performance and user experience. Non-essential cookies can be controlled in your browser where applicable.

Your Rights

  • Update your profile information through account settings.
  • Request access or correction to your personal information.
  • Contact the team for questions about collection and use.

Contact

For privacy queries relating to FUSS, email fuss.team@flinders.edu.au. For broader university privacy matters, contact the relevant university privacy contact point.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy to reflect service or legal changes. Material updates will be communicated via the platform.
