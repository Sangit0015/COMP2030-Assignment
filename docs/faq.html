Frequently Asked Questions

FUSS (Flinders Uni Skill Share) is a student platform that connects learners and helpers to share skills, collaborate across disciplines, and build confidence using a simple credit system.

Current Flinders University students. Sign in with your Flinders identity to keep the community trusted and safe.

Earn credits when you help others and spend credits when you request support. Credits are not money; they recognise contribution and encourage reciprocity.

Go to the Skills page to add, edit, or remove skills. Changes appear immediately on your profile.

Peer learning within your topic rules and academic integrity standards. Helpers may explain concepts and methods but must not complete assessments for you.

Use the Help & Support form or email fuss.team@flinders.edu.au. For immediate risks, contact emergency services first.
