Community Guidelines

Principles

  • Respect, inclusion, and courtesy for all students and disciplines.
  • Constructive peer learning and genuine skill development.
  • Safety, integrity, and accountability in every interaction.

Expected Conduct

  • Communicate clearly and respectfully in listings, chats, and sessions.
  • Be reliable with bookings; notify others promptly of changes.
  • Share accurate information and recognise the limits of your expertise.
  • Use credits fairly; avoid manipulation or coercion.

Not Allowed

  • Harassment, hate, bullying, threats, or discriminatory behaviour.
  • Sharing illegal content or infringing others’ intellectual property.
  • Fraud, spam, scams, or attempts to bypass platform safeguards.
  • Academic misconduct including contract cheating or plagiarism.

Academic Integrity

Support must comply with topic rules and university standards. Helpers may explain concepts and methods but must not complete assessment tasks for others.

Reporting and Safety

Use the Help & Support form or email fuss.team@flinders.edu.au to report concerns. For immediate risks, contact emergency services first.

Moderation

Content or accounts may be reviewed. Actions can include warnings, content removal, credit reversal where appropriate, temporary restrictions, or suspension.

Guideline Updates

These guidelines may change as the platform evolves. Updates will be communicated via the service.
